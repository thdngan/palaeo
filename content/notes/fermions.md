A particle that has a half-odd-integer spin (spin 1/2, spin 3/2, etc.)

Fermions include:
- [[notes/quarks|quarks]]
- [[notes/leptons|leptons]]
- [[notes/composite particles|composite particles]]

Composite fermions are the fundamental constituents of all matter.
